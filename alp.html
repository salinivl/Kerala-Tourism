Alappuzha

Alappuzha, famous for its boat races, backwater holidays, beaches, marine products and coir industry, is referred to as Venice of the East. Alappuzha Beach is a popular picnic spot. Another delightful experience while in Alappuzha is the houseboat cruise. Houseboats come equipped with all the comforts of a good hotel room including furnished bedrooms, modern toilets, cozy living rooms, a kitchen and even a balcony for angling. An uninterrupted view of life in the backwaters can be enjoyed while staying in a houseboat.
